The Details

What Sets Us Apart

Finnish Specification

Built to authentic Finnish sauna standards with proper thermal mass, ventilation flow, and stove-to-room ratios. Real löyly, not marketing copy.

Veteran Built

USMC veteran-owned. We bring the same discipline, attention to detail, and commitment to quality that the Corps demands — to every joint, every board, every build.

Wood-Fired Heat

We spec wood-burning stoves — Harvia, Kuuma, or equivalent — because nothing replicates the soft, radiant heat and ritual of feeding a real fire.

Backyard Ready

Designed for residential properties throughout the Bay Area. We handle design, build, and delivery — you just need a level spot and a cold plunge nearby.

Select-Grade Lumber

Hand-picked cedar and fir from Northern California suppliers. Every board is inspected — no warps, no knots where they don't belong, no shortcuts on materials.

Built to Last

Rockwool insulation. Foil vapor barrier. Stainless steel fasteners. Proper ventilation. Every material chosen for longevity — this sauna outlives the mortgage.

The Process
